The F. Libra

The Libra who sings to find some balance

Libra is balance.

Balance between black and white, strength and softness, politics and spirituality, straight rap and RnB.

Libra is the artistic name of Sónia Curcialeiro the Lisbon based singer/rapper/songwriter that brings to the international music scene a female approach on the politic conscious rap mixing it with the fragility and warmth of RnB. Libra’s music is driven by her link to ancestry and her willingness to spread social-political awareness and urgency to the discovery of the self.
